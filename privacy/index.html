1. Who we are

SiteBot is a website and lead generation platform for cell phone repair and device buyback shops. It is operated by iMobile Repair Center. When this policy says "SiteBot," "we," "us," or "our," it means SiteBot and its operators.

Our contact email is info@sitebot.tech. Our website is sitebot.tech.

2. What we collect

Waitlist signups

When you join the SiteBot waitlist we collect your name, shop name, and email address. We may also generate a referral code and record the timestamp of your signup. This information is stored in a Google Sheet managed by SiteBot.

Active customers

When you become a SiteBot customer we collect additional information needed to build and operate your shop website, including your shop name, address, phone number, services offered, and pricing data you provide via Google Sheets.

Information collected automatically

When you visit sitebot.tech we may collect standard web analytics data including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This is used to understand how the site is being used and improve it.

Communications

If you email us or contact us directly we retain those communications to follow up and improve our service.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Manage and operate the SiteBot waitlist
  • Contact waitlist members when early access spots open up
  • Build, configure, and maintain your shop website and lead system
  • Send you updates about SiteBot features, pricing, and announcements
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Improve the platform based on how it is being used
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for advertising. We do not build profiles for third-party ad targeting. We do not sell your data.

4. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information to anyone. We share information only with the third-party services needed to operate the platform:

  • Google Sheets and Google Apps Script — used to store waitlist signups and power the pricing backend for shop calculators
  • EmailJS — used to send offer code emails and lead notifications on behalf of shop owners
  • Vercel — used to host sitebot.tech and customer shop websites
  • Twilio (when SMS features are active) — used to send automated SMS follow-up messages to leads who have opted in

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data. We choose providers carefully and only share what is necessary for the service to function.

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of SiteBot, its operators, or others.

5. Information about your customers

When someone visits your SiteBot-powered shop website and uses the device quote calculator, they may provide their email address and device details to lock a price. This information belongs to you as the shop owner. SiteBot processes it on your behalf to send offer code emails and lead notifications.

As a shop owner using SiteBot you are responsible for:

  • Having a privacy policy on your own shop website that informs your customers how their data is used
  • Only using customer contact information for the purpose it was collected — following up on device offers and repair leads
  • Complying with applicable privacy laws in your jurisdiction regarding customer data
  • Obtaining proper consent before sending any SMS messages to your customers through SiteBot's SMS features

SiteBot does not use your customers' information for its own marketing purposes.

6. Cookies and analytics

Sitebot.tech may use cookies or similar technologies to remember preferences and understand how visitors use the site. We do not use advertising cookies or share analytics data with ad networks.

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect how some parts of the site function but will not prevent you from accessing content or joining the waitlist.

We do not currently use Google Analytics or any third-party analytics platform that sells or shares your browsing data with advertisers.

7. How long we keep it

Waitlist data is retained until you ask to be removed or until SiteBot ceases operations. We keep it because we may reach out about access as new spots become available.

Customer data is retained for the duration of your active subscription and for up to 12 months after cancellation for billing records and dispute resolution purposes.

You can ask us to delete your information at any time by emailing info@sitebot.tech. We will confirm deletion within 30 days except where we are required by law to retain certain records.

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Correct any information that is inaccurate
  • Delete your information from our systems
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email we send
  • Withdraw consent for SMS communications by replying STOP to any message we send

To exercise any of these rights email us at info@sitebot.tech. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in California you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom you may have additional rights under the GDPR. Contact us if you have questions about your specific rights.

9. Children

SiteBot is a business platform intended for adults who own or operate cell phone repair and buyback businesses. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor please contact us at info@sitebot.tech and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy as the platform evolves. When we do we will update the date at the top of this page. If changes are significant we will notify active customers by email before the changes take effect.

Continued use of SiteBot after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
