The shop

iMobile Repair Center has been serving Detroit since before most people knew what a buyback shop was. The store sits on Dequindre Street near 8 Mile, one of the most trafficked corridors in the city. The shop does iPhone repair, iPad repair, computer repair, and buys devices for cash. Cash, CashApp, and Zelle. Most repairs done the same day.

The store had the reviews. It had the reputation. It had customers who drove across town to sell their phones and get their screens fixed. What it did not have was a website that showed any of that to someone sitting at home searching Google at 10pm wondering where to sell their old iPhone.

The problem every shop has

A customer cracks their screen, searches "iPhone repair near me," and clicks the first result they see. If that is not your shop, it does not matter how good you are. They are already gone.

The same thing happens on the buyback side. Someone wants to sell their iPhone 14. They search "sell iPhone Detroit" or "how much is my iPhone worth." If your shop does not show up with a page that answers that question and gives them an instant number, they go to a kiosk or an online buyer and you never see that phone.

Most shops do not have the time to build a website, write SEO content, wire up a pricing calculator, and set up lead capture. They are too busy fixing screens and buying phones. That is exactly the problem SiteBot was built to solve.

The iPhone and iPad quote calculators

A customer visits the sell page, picks their model and condition, and gets an instant cash offer. No phone call. No form to submit. Just a number, right there on the screen.
