Questions

What does SiteBot actually do for my shop?
SiteBot builds and manages your entire online presence. You get a professional website, a device quote calculator that captures leads 24 hours a day, SEO articles that rank on Google for repair and buyback searches, and automated email follow-up for every lead. Everything is set up and running for you. You focus on your shop, SiteBot handles the marketing.
How is this different from RepairDesk or similar shop software?
RepairDesk and tools like it help you manage the customers you already have. They handle repair tickets, inventory, and invoicing. SiteBot gets customers to your shop in the first place. It is a marketing and lead capture system, not a point-of-sale tool. Most shops need both, but if your store is quiet, SiteBot is the problem that needs solving first.
Do I need to know how to build a website or do any technical work?
No. SiteBot builds everything for you. You answer a few questions about your shop when you sign up and we handle the design, the content, the SEO setup, and the calculator. If you want to change your prices, you update a Google Sheet and the website updates automatically. That is the most technical thing you will ever need to do.
Does this work for buyback shops, repair shops, or both?
Both. The website includes pages for your repair services and a full device buyback calculator for iPhones, iPads, and other devices. Most cell phone shops do both and the system is built with that in mind. You can turn sections on or off depending on what your shop focuses on.
