About SiteBot

Built inside a real shop.
Not a conference room.

Most software for phone repair shops is built by people who have never repaired a phone or bought a device from a walk-in customer. SiteBot is different. It was built by a shop owner who does this every day.

It started with a problem on 8 Mile

iMobile Repair Center has been buying and fixing phones in Detroit for years. The shop is on Dequindre Street near 8 Mile. Good location, good reviews, customers who come back. A business that works.

But there was a problem that kept showing up. Customers were searching Google to find a place to sell their iPhone or get their screen fixed. If your shop was not showing up in those results, you did not get the call. You did not get the walk-in. You did not buy the phone or fix the screen. It went to whoever ranked.

The shop was good. The website was not doing its job. There was no device quote calculator. No pages built to rank for "sell iPhone Detroit" or "iPhone repair near me." No system to capture a lead from someone who checked the site at midnight and left without calling.

The customers were out there. They just could not find us the way they were searching.

So the work started. Not as a software project. As a shop problem that needed solving. Build the pages. Wire up a pricing calculator. Set up lead capture. Get the Google presence right. Test everything on a real store with real customers before calling it done.

That shop is iMobile. Everything on SiteBot was tested there first.

Why this matters to you

There are plenty of tools built for phone repair shops. Most of them focus on managing the work you already have. Repair tickets, inventory, invoicing. Useful things once customers are inside your shop.

None of them focus on getting customers to your shop in the first place.

That is the gap SiteBot fills. A shop owner should not have to figure out SEO, build a calculator from scratch, set up email capture, and wire up a Google Sheet backend just to get leads from their own website. That is weeks of work most shop owners do not have time for and do not want to learn.

SiteBot builds it, runs it, and keeps it working. You focus on fixing phones and buying devices. SiteBot handles the part that gets customers to your door.
